    the suns

    the two suns at the center of the solar system. Ta is a main sequence star, about 4.5 billion years old, and provides the majority of light and heat in the solar system. Li is a neutron star, significantly smaller than Ta, and about 2.7 billion years old.

    Talador is the smallest planet in the solar system, and is mostly desertlike. unlike the other planets, it orbits only around Li, having one hot season and one cold season depending on its proximity to Ta in its orbit.

    the inner solar system

    Gaia is the most... normal1? planet in the solar system. it's a rocky ocean planet, with the bulk of its land in its northern hemisphere, and most of the land on its surface green and habitable. it doesn't have a large starfaring culture, so its contact with other planets is limited, but it does have at least one starport known to the public. i have a seperate note for the gaian continents.

    Utori is a rocky planet, tidally locked to the suns. it has one moon, also tidally locked2.
    ...not much else is known about the planet. its culture is extremely authoritarian, disallowing and demonizing space travel, and imprisoning offworld travellers who stop there.

    Eva... used to be a rocky ocean planet, like Gaia, but is currently a mass of floating islands suspended over a sea of churning mist and souls. its orbit is synced with Ril, which it had a technological treaty with before the War of Admonition. its surface was shattered during the war, and it is now home to the majority of Radiant Evarin.
    due to the nature of Eva's surface, tracking continents is nearly impossible, but i have a note for notable islands.

    an artifical asteroid belt created during the War of Admonition. the majority of material is in the plane between Eva and Ril's orbits, but it has spread out slightly over time. it is mostly made of artifical material, including ruins of starships, but it has caught natural asteroids over time.

    Ril, similarly to Eva used to be a rocky ocean planet. in the War of Admonition, its atmosphere and magnetosphere were almost completely destroyed, and its core was significantly dimmed, making it into a dark, cold planet, with breathable air only present in certain controlled zones - usually around cities.
