    • ode to the stars

    Gray as rocks below, the clouds obscure my view
    Covering up that sacred vision to let me look at you
    City lights on high, crowding out the sky
    One by one the stars go on until we say goodbye
    Brightly shines the sun, climbing from that night
    We walk and walk and when we stop we find that it's gone right
    Softly singing moon, I will find you soon
    Starshine falls on open fields and makes the flowers bloom
    Gently sighs the man, basking in the dawn
    Looking up out at the stars and placing shapes upon
