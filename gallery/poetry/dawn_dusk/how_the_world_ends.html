    • this is how the world ends

    "this is how the world ends", they told me.
    "any step out of line,
    any glance off the path,
    any desire for anything else

    can only bring it closer".

    the line was a straight-chalk path
    cut through vibrant wilderness.
    i was only allowed the white and blue-gray
    of normalcy.

    but after a while
    i think i began to see it
    the line they asked me to hold so close to my chest
    was nothing more than

    a rope around my ankle
    a collar around my neck
    a chain around my wrist
    a line.

    and they aren't here anymore, anyways.
    if i don't get to see the world,
    or if it does truly end,
    does it make much of a difference?
    to me, anyways.

    slowly
    but surely
    one step at a time
    the line disappeared from thought
    and from memory
    and the world isn't over yet.
