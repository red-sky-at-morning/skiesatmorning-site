    • a reflection

    (written: apr 29, 2025)

    i place my hand on the mirror
    reaching for something beyond

    i know not what i search for, but
    i search the reflection's face,
    knowing it is not mine

    he stares at me
    unflinching, unwavering
    she offers a hand

    i do not take it
    a way out
    they offer me

    i know who i am
    so as i turn around,
    i look at the reflection one last time
