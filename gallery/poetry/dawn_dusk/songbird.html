    • songbird

    (written: apr 3, 2025)

    it sat,
    still as a leaf on a bright summer's day,
    until,
    suddenly,
    it fluttered,
    and flapped,
    and jumped its way
    into clear blue sky,
    disappearing over the distant horizon,
    dipped in rich blue moonlight,
    singing all the while
