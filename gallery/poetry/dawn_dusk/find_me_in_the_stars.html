    • find me in the stars

    (written: april 4, 2025)

    dip me into starlight,
    and find when you pull me back out
    that i am different than i was before

    cover me in stardust,
    and when the haze and dust all clears,
    see that maybe you never truly knew me at all

    tell me to reach for the moon,
    and you will lose me to the night sky,
    the stars calling my name and speckling my skin

    call out to dark skies,
    looking for the person i used to be,
    and i will whisper, *you are looking in all the wrong places*

    find me in the stars -
    for you will never find me anyplace else -
    and maybe you will see what you were missing before
