    • songbird

    (written: apr 29, 2025)

    scraping, crawling,
    something matted and grimy
    crawled its way through the muck
    and dirt
    to find me here

    where else has it been?
    i reach out,
    and it shows me

    it crawls through
    the cramped and broken city streets,
    chews its way through
    worn-out old barricades,
    smashes through
    crushing walls and pressure,

    all to find me here.
    when hope finds me
    in every dark place,

    i do not send it away
    for i know where it has been
    and i know it will only return.
