    • dawn//dusk

    (written: apr 28, 2025)

    it's strange how hard endings can be,
    for endings never truly are endings,
    only the start of something else,

    like when the songbird's chick
    spreads its wings for the first time,
    and does not fall,
    but soars,

    or how the stardust all clears,
    and you can see that she was always
    someone you loved,

    or that every end of the world
    never really was that for everyone,
    just the ones that wanted it to be,

    or how the end of the day
    bleeds into night
    bleeds back into day,
    bleeds back into night,

    the colors of twilight
    painting a neverending picture
    of dawn and dusk.
