    • insignificance

    (written: april 3, 2025)

    this blue marble,
    so large, but still
    insignificant
    when compared to
    everything else

    entire lives,
    lived without knowing
    what else could be.
    insignificant,
    yet still grand.

    towering spires of
    passion,
    and calculated love,
    when viewed from up here,
    vanish from sight.

    but meaning is given not by their size
    but by the effort afforded to create them.

    insignificant,
    yet not.
