    • love//hope

    (written: apr 28, 2025)

    hope and love are
    very similar.

    and yet i know the shape of hope
    much better than the shape of love.

    hope is a sword,
    a candle,
    something to fight back darkness.
    it is something with claws,
    and sharp teeth,
    and a score to settle
    with apathy.

    i know how to wield hope.
    i do not know how to wield love.
    where hope is sharp and fierce,
    love is soft, and kind.

    but i cannot mistake its softness for docility,
    and kindness is a very powerful weapon
    when wielded correctly.

    i have fought apathy
    many times before,
    and yet it returns
    time and time again.

    i have fought with hope,
    but not its dual,
    for self-criticism is far easier
    than self-love

    but i do know how to love
    for i can look out
    at the green fields
    and deep night
    and find
    i want to live

    i have not many words left to say,
    so i look inwards,
    and grant myself this:
    i love y
