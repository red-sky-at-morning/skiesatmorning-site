    • i will not

    (written: apr 8, 2025)

    no,
    i am not sorry.
    i have nothing to apologize for.
    not to you.

    what would you have me say?
    this self belongs not to you,
    but me.
    you have never controlled me.

    i finally feel
    free
    and you ask me
    to be sorry?

    so
    no,
    i will not
    apologize

    not for being
    true to myself
    in spite
    of you
