    (written: jun 5, 2026)

    look into its mirrored surface
    it reflects my face
    but i can't find recognition

    watch as it falls from the leaf
    a tiny pool forming in the grass
    the reflection ripples
    and the drop reforms

    gaze through it again
    watch how it shifts as i do
    each change a new person
    maybe now i can see myself
