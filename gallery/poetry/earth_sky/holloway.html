    (written: jun 2, 2026)

    i walk through the forest
    on a path yet unknown
    through the verdant underbrush

    there is a path, of course,
    but i know what i want
    and i know where i am going
    so i push ahead nonetheless


    it is easier the second time
    my feet know the path,
    and the plants know my tread

    it is still not a path true
    not yet, anyways
    but it is still familiar
    as i walk through again


    it is not the third time through
    that i realize
    a path has formed

    the forest shies away,
    bare dirt and roots
    all that the forest is formed of
    soft under my feet


    i have lost count
    of the times i have walked the path
    but it would not have become this
    if i had not

    the earth gives refuge
    to what was once a harsh journey,
    the once-hostile plants
    climbing over and down the sides

    the choice i made-
    over, and over,
    to get here

    i think it was worth it
