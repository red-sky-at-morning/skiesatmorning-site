    • sunflower

    (written: jun 1, 2026)

    sunrise, soft in the east
    over a sea of golden yellow
    each an individual its own,
    yet each facing the same

    noon, high overhead
    the bright yellow petals
    drink the lifeblood so kindly offered
    small, and yet so large

    sunset, a farewell
    the night is cold
    and unforgiving

    but they know that it is not forever
    and they turn to the east again.
