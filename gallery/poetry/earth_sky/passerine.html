    • passerine

    (written: jun 12, 2026)

    the sun sets
    over the treeline of the forest
    i sit, watching
    as it paints the sky in pastel hues

    it is peaceful here,
    but maybe
    a little lonely
    the forest is comforting
    but not company

    maybe that's why
    we both could feel
    our hearts beating in sync,
    one, then another

    then, you were there
    sitting beside me
    as silent as the forest
    that connected us

    in the silence,
    i feel like a passerine bird
    not unable to interrupt,
    always free to leave
    but unwilling

    so we sit
    watching the sunset
    over the treeline of our forest
