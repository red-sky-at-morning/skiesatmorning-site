    (written: jun 3, 2026)

    in the woods behind my house
    there's a dead tree
    leaning over the path

    every time i walk under
    i wonder if it will fall this time

    i wonder if the sprouts
    living in its pale shadow
    somehow feel the same

    i wonder if the dead tree
    somewhere in its forgotten afterlife
    can feel it too

    the sword of a life
    lived to the fullest
    held over those who remain

    i wonder if i'm the only person who's thought about this.

    .

    they cut it down two weeks ago
    i guess nothing lasts forever.
