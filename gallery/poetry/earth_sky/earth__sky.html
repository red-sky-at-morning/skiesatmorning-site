    is as different from
    the sky
    as completely possible
    it is near where it is far,
    steadfast where it is fickle,
    red where it is blue

    the sky
    is as different from
    the earth
    as completely possible
    it is far where it is near,
    fickle where it is steadfast,
    blue where it is red

    but
    they still meet at the horizon
    together as one
    only at infinity

    the earth
    as they first appear
    for it can be near all the same,
    steadfast, when it cares to

    blue, like the vast ocean

    just as well as it can be
