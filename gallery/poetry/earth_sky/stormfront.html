    (written: jun 6, 2026)

    in it comes again
    effortless, inevitable
    as surely as the storm it heralds

    it is an omen to some
    of dark times; of destruction
    but i feel how it tosses my hair
    and stirs the trees around me

    and i think not of the touch of a foe
    instead i feel that of a lover

    so i stand, heeding her warning
    but not her instruction

    as the rain falls on my skin
    i think
    what else can this be
    if not love?
