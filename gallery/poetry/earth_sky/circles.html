    (written: jun 7, 2026)

    there
    off the leaf
    a drop falls

    into a ring
    of fungi
    growing,
    consuming

    not the cause
    of the decay
    nor because of it

    their roots
    span the forest
    everything connected
    helping the others

    they give
    food
    and water
    and warning of danger

    like the water
    passing through the roots
    now coalesced into
    a stormcloud overhead

    ready to pour down
    but first
    watch
