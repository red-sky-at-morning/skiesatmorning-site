    • passerby

    (written: jun 4, 2026)

    gaze out from the underbrush
    watch the shoes of people walking by
    wish i could be like them

    it should be easy
    the path is open
    and safe
    and the ground is soft
    (far softer than in the forest)

    but
    every time i try
    something stops me
    the freedom does not feel like
    security safety

    are they the same?
    are we
    the same?

    i don't think i can ever know
    as long as i stay here

    the world won't end if i'm wrong, anyways.
