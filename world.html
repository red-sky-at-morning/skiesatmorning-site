    you wake in a strange place. the first thing you notice is the hard-packed dirt underneath your hands, as you come to in a half-sitting pose. the room is well-lit, with flickering sconces on the thick log walls. one wall is covered entirely with several large maps, with pins pushed into them at locations you can't make out. there's a small window in the other wall, with several books open on a sturdy desk underneath it. a spark of red light flits over the pages, struggling to turn one as you watch.

    the sprite flickers over its books, seemingly having missed your presence. as you begin to move, it jolts suddenly - startled, possibly - before floating closer to you. it pulses as it speaks.

    oh! i wasn't expecting visitors...

    i'm, um- i'm called sunsprite

    my court, they- they asked me to...
    i have it somewhere around here...

    she darts back over to the desk, poking around the papers strewn around. after a tick, it picks up a scrap of paper and flies back over to you, considerably slower this time.

    'you are not to return to the library of the court of stars until such a time as you have researched and learned all there is to know of the planes and the peoples that live therein'

    she lets the paper drop into your lap.

    so i've been... learning everything i can. every place, location, the people that live there...
    it's been going kinda slow.

    there's a groundskeeper that comes around sometimes that.. helps me stay organized.
    it's very hard to move these books...

    but, um, feel free to look around! it.. might help me finish my task sooner!
