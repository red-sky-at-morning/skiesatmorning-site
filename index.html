    istera has been my passion project since junior year of high school. it's a space for me to play around in (basically just me going 'hey wouldn't that be cool'). it started with three main pillars (that have since been expanded, but):

    • History is young, and magic is new.

      much of what used to be known has been lost in wars or other calamities. there lies much still to find in the world.

    • Magic comes from belief.

      whether that be a sacred wishing star a thousand years past, or a humble pet rock, belief can form powerful magic with time.

    • The Gods are dead, or something close.

      the old gods are dead, at the hands of their worshippers. magic runs wild and free, open to any who can grasp it.
